Lasagna Recipe

The final product: Lasagna

This dish features a delicious and nutritious portion of ground beef followed by a citric complimentation of the tomato sauce.
    Ingredients
  • Tomatos
  • Ground beef
  • Cheese
    Steps
  1. Cook the ground beef and season to liking
  2. Layer lasagna, ground beef tomato sauce, lasagna and cheese until satisfied
  3. Cook at 400f for 45 minutes or until cheese is golden brown
